AI-Assisted Interview System
for Job Matching

A study prototype for BISU Clarin graduating students that simulates interview responses, extracts relevant skills, compares NLP models, and recommends suitable entry-level job opportunities.

Get Started
1
Interview Simulation
Graduating students answer common entry-level interview questions through voice or text.
2
Model Comparison
The prototype compares all-MiniLM-L6-v2, all-mpnet-base-v2, and DistilBERT for skill extraction.
3
Job Matching
Matching Accuracy, Fallback Rate, and Containment Rate are highlighted alongside job recommendations.
